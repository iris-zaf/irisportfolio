About Me

Back in 2020, when the world hit pause and we all got way too familiar with our living room furniture, I decided to do something wild: I taught myself to code. I would say it was the est decision I ever made, but let’s be honest, it was more like a series of happy accidents and late-night YouTube marathons.

Today, I’m a web developer with a passion for clean code, creative designs, and solving real-world problems — all sparked by a global lockdown.

When I’m not building websites, I’m probably working on new ideas or searching for the new Marathon event. 👩‍💻✨🏃‍♀️
